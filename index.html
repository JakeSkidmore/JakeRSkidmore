Professional Biography

Jake Skidmore is a seasoned engineering and operations professional with a diverse background spanning technical, managerial, and customer service roles. His career began through a vocational work program during high school, where he demonstrated an early aptitude for technical problem-solving at STS Automotive Repair (2009-2013).

Driven by professional ambition, Jake pursued automotive engineering certifications and enrolled at Brookdale Community College, where he excelled academically, graduating at the top of his class. His pursuit of higher education continued at Thomas Edison State University, where he initially focused on Automotive Technology before transitioning to Technical Studies as his career interests evolved toward engineering.

At Colonial Airstream (2013-2017), Jake progressed from technician to Service Representative Manager, distinguishing himself through mechanical expertise, customer service excellence, and leadership abilities. During this period, he earned his Master's in Engineering Management from Drexel University, a pivotal achievement that led to his recruitment by Crestron Electronics.

At Crestron, Jake established himself as a subject matter expert across product lines and design engineering. His leadership transformed the Sales Support Service Department, where he elevated team performance, reduced lead times by 73% (from 15 to 4 days), and successfully rehabilitated a key global client relationship. His tenure has been marked by managing high-profile projects and consistently delivering operational improvements.

Currently seeking new opportunities, Jake brings 12+ years of experience in engineering management, process optimization, and team leadership. His career demonstrates a pattern of rapid skill acquisition, performance transformation, and the ability to drive measurable business results. He is particularly interested in roles that leverage his technical background, management expertise, and proven ability to enhance organizational efficiency.
